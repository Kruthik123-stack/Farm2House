Important Instructions

  • Select Category
    • Choose Vegetable or Fruit from the first dropdown.
  • Select Crop
    • After selecting the category, the crop list will appear,Pick the correct crop from the list.
  • Enter Quantity
    • Type the quantity in kilograms, Add More Crops (If Needed), Click Add to enter more crops.
  • Remove Mistakes
    • If you made a mistake, click Remove next to the wrong entry.
  • Submit And CHECK SUMMARY TABLE
  • If everything looks good, click Final Submit. You will go to the Orders Received page. Done!
